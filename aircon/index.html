When choosing the right air conditioner for your needs, you must consider several key factors to ensure you select a efficient, cost-effective, and suitable unit for your space. Capacity:The capacity of an air conditioner is measured in tons, which refers to the amount of heat the AC can remove from a room in an hour. Energy Efficiency: Energy efficiency is indicated by the star rating of an air conditioner, with higher stars representing greater efficiency. Price:The price of an air conditioner can vary widely based on capacity, features, brand, and energy efficiency.
